## Week 1–2: Arrays & Sliding Window

**Practice Problems:**

- Linked List Cycle

- Search in Rotated Sorted Array

**Practice Problems:**

- Subsets
- Permutations

**Practice Problems:**

## Week 6: Linked Lists

- Merge Two Lists

## Week 7: Stacks & Queues
- Monotonic Stack
- Next Greater Element

**Practice Problems:**

## Week 8: Trees & Binary Trees
- DFS (Preorder, Inorder, Postorder)
- BFS (Level Order)

**Practice Problems:**
- Maximum Depth of Binary Tree
- Diameter of Binary Tree
- Lowest Common Ancestor

## Week 9: Graphs
- BFS & DFS
- Topological Sort
- Union Find

**Practice Problems:**
- Number of Islands
- Course Schedule

## Week 10: Dynamic Programming
- 1D DP (Fibonacci, Climbing Stairs)
- 2D DP (Knapsack, LCS)

**Practice Problems:**
- House Robber
- Longest Increasing Subsequence
- Coin Change

## Final Days: Revision + Mock Interviews
- Revise all patterns
- Solve mixed problems daily
- Take timed mock interviews

## Tips
- Focus on patterns, not just problems
